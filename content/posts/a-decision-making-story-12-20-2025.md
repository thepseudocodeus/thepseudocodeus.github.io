## Poker Simulates Life

Current model of education provides problem solving fundamentals and practice to succeed in the real world. Many institutions choose to simplify problems in order to expose students to more concepts across more domains. This makes sense if we step back and think abstractly about the role of education: to prepare millions of young people to add value to a constantly changing global economy. When viewed this way the complexity of the problem is more clear.

Students do not know what they will do throughout their careers. We do not know what the economy will be comprised of and need.
